Gear Up for Good
The Blue Ridge YMCA is proud to announce its upcoming gently used sports 
equipment drive, Gear Up for Good. Let’s level the playing field and help more kids 
play sports this year!
•Donate Gear: July 7 – July 21
 Bring your new or gently used sports equipment to the Blue Ridge YMCA, Cedar 
Creek Family YMCA, or Harbor Point Family YMCA. 
•We Accept Equipment for:
Baseball
Tennis
Yoga
Bikes
Basketball
Swimming
Surfing
Skateboards
Soccer
Golf
Boxing
Cheerleading
   *Note: We do not accept sports clothing or swimwear.
•Shop the Sale: July 25 (2 PM – 5 PM)
 All donated sports equipment will be available for purchase at the Blue Ridge 
YMCA. Community members are invited to shop the sale for affordable, quality 
sports gear!
VOLUNTEER!
We are looking for volunteers to help organize donations and prepare for the sale.
