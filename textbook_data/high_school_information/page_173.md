다음 글에 드러난 Noah의 심경 변화로 가장 적절한 것은? 
Noah sat on the park bench, watching people set up telescopes. His parents had brought 
him to see a meteor shower, but he didn’t care much. He had never thought the night sky 
was interesting. Stars were just tiny dots, and he didn’t see why people were so excited. 
He folded his arms and stared at the ground, waiting for time to pass. Around him, voices 
buzzed with excitement, but he barely listened. Then, a sudden wave of surprise spread 
through the crowd. He looked up. A bright line of light shot across the sky, then another. 
The meteors burned fast, disappearing in seconds. His eyes widened. How often did this 
happen? Could he predict the next one? Were there more coming? He leaned forward, 
scanning every corner of the sky. The stars, once meaningless, now held his full attention. 
For the first time, he truly wanted to see more.
