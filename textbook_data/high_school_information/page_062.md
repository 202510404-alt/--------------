School Bell Sound Contest
Wakeville High School is holding a school bell sound contest. The theme of this 
contest is Happy School Life.
Deadline: December 12, 2025
Participants: Wakeville High School students of all grades
Application Details
• Submit entries in MP3 format only. 
• Upload entries to our school website (file size limit: 1MB).
• Include the description of the sound creation process.
Selection Method
• 
 
 Three 
school bell sounds will be selected by student vote, based on creativity and 
suitability.
Awards
• 
 
 The winning school bell sounds will replace our current bell sounds.
•  
 The winners of the contest will each get a wireless speak
er.
